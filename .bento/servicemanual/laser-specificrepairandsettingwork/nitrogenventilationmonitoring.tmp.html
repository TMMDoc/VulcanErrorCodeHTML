Check the sealing gas pressure at the cutting unit KL59

For L99, sealing gas is only available from a laser power of 6 kW with KL59.

  1. In the HMI, select “Controls”.

  2. Press the monitoring button. .Fig. 3‑101

    |

  1. Select “Trumpf KL59”.

  2. Read off the sealing gas pressure. Nominal value: 40 ± 5 mbar. .Fig. 3‑102
