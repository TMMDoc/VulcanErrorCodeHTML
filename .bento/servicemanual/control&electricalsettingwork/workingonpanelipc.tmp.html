Replacing the Panel IPC

  • The machine is connected to the network.

  • Vulcan system is still accessible (if not, please contact SCC teams to release the license). Means, Tools, Materials

  • Standard tool set, mat. no. 0135600.

    1. Go to HMI and click the “wrench” icon. Release the license

    2. Press “License”. .Fig. 5‑99

      |

  1. Note down the license serial key and the machine number. .Fig. 5‑100

    |

  1. Press the “Release license” button.

    • The machine must be connected to the network.

  2. Switch off the machine.

  3. Detach the protective cover for the IPC. Remove the Panel IPC .Fig. 5‑101

    |

  1. Disconnect all the cables and make some marks if necessary.

  2. Remove the protective cover for the cables. .Fig. 5‑102

    |

  1. Undo the screws for the protective box and place it onto the X2 axis.

    • Pay attention to the length limit of the cables. .Fig. 5‑103

      |

  1. Loosen all the screws used to fix the Panel IPC. .Fig. 5‑104

    |

  1. Take the old Panel IPC off from the machine.

  2. Place the old Panel IPC on the table.

  3. Remove the back cover of the old Panel IPC. Replace the Panel IPC

    • Another two screws on the left. .Fig. 5‑105

      |

  1. Disconnect the cables for the SATA SSD. .Fig. 5‑106

    | |

|1 MSATA SSD |2 SATA SSD

  1. Undo the 4x screws for SATA SSD and take if off.

  2. Undo the screw for MSATA SSD and take it off.

  3. Fit the two SSD to the new Panel IPC.

  4. To install the new Panel IPC, perform the removal steps in the reverse order.

  5. Switch on the machine and go to HMI.

  6. Register Vulcan according to the machine number and license.

    • An internet connection is needed for registration. .Fig. 5‑107

      |

Replacing the SSD

  • The machine is already switched off. Means, Tools, Materials

  • Standard tool set, mat. no. 0135600.

    1. Take the Panel IPC off from the machine (see “Replacing thePanel IPC”, pg. xxx). Remove the SSD

  • As for replacing the hard disk, it’s unnecessary to releasethe license.

    1. Remove the back cover of the Panel IPC.

  • Another two screws on the left. .Fig. 5‑108

    |

  1. Disconnect the cables for the SATA SSD. .Fig. 5‑109

    | |

|1 MSATA SSD |2 SATA SSD

  1. Undo the 4x screws for SATA SSD and take if off.

  2. Undo the screw for MSATA SSD and take it off.

  3. Fit the new SSD. Fit the new SSD

  4. To install the Panel IPC, perform the removal steps in the reverse order.

  5. Switch on the machine.

  6. If the system startup disk (usually MSATA SSD) is replaced, restore the whole system (see “Back up/Restore the whole system”, pg. xxx).

  7. If the disk used for backup (usually SATA SSD) is replaced, back up the whole system (see “Back up/Restore the whole system”, pg. xxx).

  • Standard tool set, mat. no. 0135600.

    1. Take the Panel IPC off from the machine (see “Replacing thePanel IPC”, pg. xxx).

  • As for replacing the hard disk, it’s unnecessary to releasethe license.

    1. Remove the back cover of the Panel IPC.

  • Another two screws on the left. .Fig. 5‑110

    |

  1. Replace the battery under the black tape:

    • Red cable connected to the positive pole of battery.

    • Black cable connected to the negative pole of battery.

    • Apply insulation tape to the battery to fix the black and red cables. .Fig. 5‑111
