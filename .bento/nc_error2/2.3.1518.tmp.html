Description

G2/G distance between starting and destination center points is implausible with regard to the other entries

Cause

The arc is programmed inaccurately. The radius of the arc at the starting point differs from the radius at the target point.

Action

There are two possible solutions, either: Program circular arc correctly in CAD/CAM system, or increase the tolerance to the programmed inaccuracy (see machine constant MK_CFG_POSITION_TOLERANCE_MM , old notation: MK_EPSILONMM)
